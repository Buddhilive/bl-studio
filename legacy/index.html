Buddhi Live Studio aka BL Studio is an Artificial Intelligence powered music generation toolkit. You can generate Music ideas using this toolkit and download the generated music as MIDI. Then you can import these MIDI files to your favorite DAW where you can develop a great piece of music with your own creativity.

Even if you are not a musician, you can play with these tools and have fun enjoying the artificial intelligence generated music. The toolkit is completely free to use. Our intension is not to replace musicians with an Artificial Intelligence, it’s to create an assistant that with help musicians to compose great piece of music.

Multitrack

Generates a melody with multiple tracks

Trios

Generates Trios (melody, bass, and drums)

Groove

Generates Grooves with impressive performances

Melodica

Generates a melody idea to start a music project
